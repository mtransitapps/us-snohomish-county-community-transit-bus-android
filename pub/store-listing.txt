Full description:
This app adds Snohomish County Community Transit buses information to MonTransit.

This app provides the buses schedule as well as the latest news from communitytransit.blogspot.com, YouTube and @MyCommTrans on Twitter.

Community Transit buses serve Snohomish County, Washington, United States.

Once this application is installed, the MonTransit app will display buses information (schedule...).

This application only has a temporary icon and no screen: download the MonTransit app (free) in the "More ..." section bellow or by following this Google Play link http://goo.gl/pCk5mV

You can install this application on the SD card but it is not recommended.

The information comes from the GTFS file provided by Sound Transit.
http://www.soundtransit.org/Developer-resources/Data-downloads

This application is free and open-source:
https://github.com/mtransitapps/us-snohomish-county-community-transit-bus-android

This app is not related with Community Transit and Sound Transit.

Permissions:
- Other: required to read the news from communitytransit.blogspot.com, Twitter & YouTube
